About Nafiur Agro

Our mission, vision, and commitment to sustainable agriculture

Our Story

Nafiur Agro was established in 2015 with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize agriculture in Bangladesh through sustainable practices and innovative solutions.

Our founder, Nafiur Rahman, grew up in a farming family in rural Bangladesh. After completing his agricultural studies, he recognized the challenges faced by local farmers and decided to create a company that would bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and modern agricultural science.

What began as a small seed supply business has grown into a comprehensive agricultural solutions provider, serving thousands of farmers across Bangladesh.

Our Mission

To empower Bangladeshi farmers with sustainable agricultural solutions that enhance productivity, improve livelihoods, and preserve the environment for future generations.

Our Vision

To be the leading agricultural innovation company in Bangladesh, transforming the nation's farming landscape through responsible practices and cutting-edge solutions.

Our Values

Integrity, sustainability, innovation, community, and excellence guide everything we do at Nafiur Agro.
