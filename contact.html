Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase seeds from Nafiur Agro?

You can purchase our seeds by visiting our head office in Dhaka, through our authorized distributors across Bangladesh, or by placing an order through our contact form. For bulk orders, please contact our sales team directly.

Do you provide agricultural consultation services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive agricultural consultation services. Our team of experts can help with crop selection, soil testing, pest management, irrigation planning, and more. You can arrange for a consultation by contacting our office.

Are your products certified organic?

Many of our products are certified organic. We work with internationally recognized certification bodies to ensure our organic products meet global standards. Look for the organic certification label on our product packaging.

Do you offer training programs for farmers?

Yes, we regularly conduct training programs for farmers across Bangladesh. These programs cover modern farming techniques, organic practices, pest management, and more. Check our website for upcoming training schedules or contact us for custom training sessions.
